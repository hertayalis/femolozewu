Gwyneth Paltrow Is So Chic in a White Strapless Dress and Layers of Swarovski Crystals

Gwyneth Paltrow was a vision in white last night as she supported Kim Kardashian’s new collaboration with Swarovski.

The Goop founder attended the Swarovski x Skims collaboration launch party, which also celebrated the opening of Swarovski’s new flagship store on New York City’s Fifth Avenue.

For the fete, Paltrow looked ethereal in a white strapless Norma Kamali dress with a cupped bra and a subtly pleated skirt that fell above her ankles. Paltrow, styled by Elizabeth Saltzman, wore the piece simply with pointy white pumps with bent heels, by Ferragamo, and layers of white Swarovski crystal necklaces, including one that wrapped around her waist like a body chain.

The former actor kept her makeup rosy and natural, wore her blonde straight hair down, and opted for a glossy baby-pink manicure.

Gotham//Getty Images

Other notable guests at the event included La La Anthony, Billy Porter, Emma Roberts, Ashley Graham, and Camila Coelho.

Meanwhile, the belle of the ball—Kardashian—showed up to the bash in a top and miniskirt set made entirely out of Swarovski crystals. Underneath the see-through pieces, she wore a pair of her now sold-out Swarovski x Skims ribbed boxer shorts.

Kardashian opened up about partnering with Swarovski in an interview with Bazaar. “The collection is meant to be versatile, and there are pieces for any preference or occasion,” she said. “The collaboration can be styled in multiple ways for every body, which is what Skims is all about.”

She added that the “sparkle and glamour of Swarovski’s crystals” has always brought her joy, and that she hopes the new collection will do the same for others.
