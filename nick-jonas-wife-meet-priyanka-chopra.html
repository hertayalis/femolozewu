Nick Jonas Wife: Meet Priyanka Chopra

Nick Jonas wife-American actor and singer, Nicholas Jerry Jonas was born on September 16th, 1992 in Dallas, Texas in the United States.

Jonas was born to Denise Miller and Paul Kelvin Jonas. He shares the same parents with his three brothers; Frankie Jonas, Kevin Jonas and Joe Jonas.

Jonas was brought up in Wyckoff, New Jersey, and his mother taught him at home. Jonas’ career began when, at the age of six, he was identified by a professional show business manager while his mother was having her hair cut in a barbershop.

At the age of seven, he started playing on Broadway, taking such parts as Gavroche, Tiny Tim, Chip Potts, and Little Jake, among others.

While performing in Beauty and the Beast (2002), Jonas co-wrote the song “Joy to the World (A Christmas Prayer)” with his father. The song was included on the compilation album Broadway’s Greatest Gifts: Carols for a Cure, Vol. 4.

Later, INO Records distributed the song to Christian radio in 2003, serving as Jonas’ lead single.

Nick Jonas career

When Jonas was seven years old, he started performing on Broadway. In 2002, he recorded his debut single, which drew the attention of Columbia Records.

The label then signed Jonas and his elder brothers, Joe and Kevin, to the Jonas Brothers band.

It’s About Time, the group’s debut studio album, was made available by the Columbia label in 2006.

The trio released their self-titled second studio album in 2007, which became their breakout album, after leaving Columbia Records and joining with Hollywood Records.

During this time, the group rose to prominence on the Disney Channel, gaining a sizable fan base through the network.

They also starred in two of their own series, Jonas (2009–2010) and Jonas Brothers: Living the Dream (2008–2010), as well as the hugely popular musical television movies Camp Rock (2008) and Camp Rock 2: The Final Jam (2010).

The trio continued to enjoy financial success with their third studio album, A Little Bit Longer (2008); the album’s lead song, “Burnin’ Up,” peaked at number five on the Billboard Hot 100 chart.

The Jonas Brothers announced a break after the release of their fourth studio album, Lines, Vines and Trying Times (2009).

In 2010, Jonas created a new group called Nick Jonas & the Administration, who issued the album Who I Am.

After that, Jonas turned his attention to acting and appeared in several Broadway productions as well as recurrent roles on the television program Smash.

After the band’s formal breakup in 2013, Jonas started working on his second solo studio album. He signed with Island Records and issued Nick Jonas through the company in 2014, the year that saw the single “Jealous” become a commercial success.

Later, together with the Island and Hollywood labels, Jonas co-founded the record company Safehouse Records.

Jonas received the Hal David Starlight Award from the Songwriters Hall of Fame in 2016 after the album’s release.

In 2017, Jonas made an appearance in the action comedy Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle, which went on to become the year’s fifth highest-grossing movie.

Early in 2019, The Jonas Brothers came back together, and in June of that year, Happiness Begins was their first studio album. On March 12, 2021, Jonas’ third solo studio album, titled Spaceman, was made available.

Last Year Was Complicated (2016), his third studio album, reached its top position at number two on the US Billboard 200 chart.

Who is Nick Jonas married to?

Jonas is married to the Indian actress, Priyanka Chopra Jonas. They started dating in May 2018 and he popped up the big question on July 19th, 2018.

They got engaged in August 2018 in a Punjabi Roka ceremony in Mumbai. In December 2018, the couple married at Umaid Bhawan Palace, Jodhpur, in traditional Hindu and Christian ceremonies.
