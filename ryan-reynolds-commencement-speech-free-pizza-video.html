Ryan Reynolds Delivers Commencement Speech to Alma Mater via YouTube

  • Ryan Reynolds delivered a graduation speech via video to students at his old alma mater.
  • The Deadpool actor also revealed, "Every grad gets one large pizza on me."
  • Reynolds joked, "I know that as soon as you saw me you were expecting dick jokes."

While the world continues to quarantine, many students are missing their graduations. Despite not being able to deliver his speech in person, Ryan Reynolds took to YouTube to share a commencement speech with graduates at this old high school.

Addressing Kitsilano Secondary School's class of 2020, Reynolds said, "You've heard the expression 'divide and conquer.' You see it everywhere. And dividing people is just a means to distract them, disarm them, whereby giving one an opportunity to conquer, and the world seems to subsist on this idea more and more and more. And it's getting really fucking boring. It's just overdone and I think that you guys want to be stylish and ahead of the curve. And I believe that your generation will be. So maybe practice the opposite, practice empathy."

The actor continued, "Some of you might consider me successful. I don't know some of you might've seen Green Lantern, but I'll tell you this: empathy has gotten me so much more, so much farther." He continued, "To my surprise [empathy] has made me money, friends, priceless memories. It's allowed me to fully accept and provide love. It's helped me recognize the mistakes I've made and learn from them. Above all it made me happy. It's something I'll probably be working on my whole life."

Finally, Reynolds offered to buy each and every graduate a large pizza to celebrate their achievements. Basically, the actor tried to make quarantine a little better for some of the students missing their graduations during the coronavirus pandemic, which is pretty adorable.
