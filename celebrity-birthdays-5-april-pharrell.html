Celebrity birthdays 5 April: Pharrell Williams, Lily James, Hayley Atwell

The weather seems to finally be getting a little better (be honest, who has forgotten what sunshine feels like?), and we hope these celebrities will be making the most of the ever-so-slightly improved conditions on their birthdays! From Disney princesses to singing superstars, we can imagine these birthday boys and girls will be marking their special days in very different ways, and we can't wait to see what they'll get up to. In the meantime, let's take a look at which celebrities we need to send our many happy returns to, what age they are turning, and what they have been up to in the lead-up to becoming another year older and wiser! Pharrell Williams

The singing star is turning 45, but will he even notice his birthday, or will he be far too busy with his triplets?! The dad-of-four welcomed three bouncing babies with his wife, Helen Lasichanh, in January 2017, but the pair have opted to keep the tots out of the limelight, and haven't revealed their names or genders. They also share a 12-year-old son, Rocket.

Pharrell will turn 45

Lily James

Mamma Mia! Lily is turning 29 ahead of the release of the much anticipated Mamma Mia 2 in July, where she plays a younger version of Meryl Streep's character, Donna Sheridan. The actress, who also starred in the Oscar-winning film His Darkest Hour, could well be spending the day celebrating with her partner, Doctor Who actor Matt Smith.

Lily will turn 29

Hayley Atwell

Hayley will be turning 36, and the star is sure to celebrate her day in style! The actress, who is perhaps best known for her role as Peggy Carter in the Marvel franchise, has been busy since her last appearance in in the role in Ant Man, most recently appearing in the BBC adaptation of Howards End as Margaret Schlegel.

Hayley will turn 36
