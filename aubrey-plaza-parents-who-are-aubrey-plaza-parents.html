Aubrey Plaza Parents: Who Are Aubrey Plaza Parents?

Aubrey Plaza Parents: Who Are Aubrey Plaza Parents?: Aubrey Plaza, officially known as Aubrey Christina Plaza is an American actress, comedian, and producer.

She developed a love for entertainment at a younger age and has been consistent throughout her career. Plaza began her career performing improv and sketch comedy at the Upright Citizens Brigade Theater.

She also performed stand-up and has appeared at the Laugh Factory and The Improv. Plaza had her first leading film role in Safety Not Guaranteed and starred in the online series The Jeannie Tate Show and on ESPN’s Mayne Street as Robin Gibney.

She appeared in the first episode of “Terrible Decisions with Ben Schwartz” on Funny or Die. Plaza is also known for her role as April Ludgate on the NBC sitcom Parks and Recreation from 2009–2015.

Plaza has appeared in several films and series including; Mystery Team, Funny People, Scott Pilgrim vs. the World, The To Do List, Life After Beth, Mike & Dave Need Wedding Dates, Child’s Play, and Happiest Season.

Plaza has also produced and starred in the films; The Little Hours, Ingrid Goes West, Black Bear, and Emily the Criminal. In 2022 she starred in the 2nd season of the HBO anthology series The White Lotus, for which she received a Golden Globe Award nomination.

That same year (2022), Plaza was given the Artist of Distinction Award at the Newport Beach Film Festival on October 16. Due to dedication and consistency, she has risen through the ranks to be one of the most sought-after acts in the entertainment space.
