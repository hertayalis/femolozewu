She Died In a Treadmill Accident, Not By Drowning, According To Mike Tysons Daughter

Exodus was reportedly discovered unconscious after strangling herself with a treadmill machine cable by Mike Tyson’s son, Miguel Tyson. The seven-year-old youngster told his parents right away.

On May 25, 2009, the sad tragedy took place. The mother of Exodus attempted CPR and called 911. The little child was brought to the hospital and kept alive by a machine.

Mike Tyson hurried back to Phoenix, Arizona from Las Vegas. Sadly, at 11:45 a.m. the next day, doctors declared Exodus Tyson dead. She was injured to death.

Rumors once circulated that Exodus had drowned and died. However, these are all only unfounded conjectures.

Phoenix Police Department spokesman Sgt. Andy Hill confirmed during a news conference that Exodus was only fiddling with the cables. She wasn’t on a treadmill at the time of the accident, and neither was the equipment in use.

The Tyson family made a statement after the event. They said that no words could adequately express the tragedy of losing their beloved Exodus.

Additionally, the family begged that their privacy be respected at this trying time.
