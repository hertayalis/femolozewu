Tori Keeth Shocks 'VPR' Viewers by Kissing Tom Schwartz and Katie Maloney in New Trailer

Credit: Bravo

The season 11 trailer of Vanderpump Rules told us many things, but mostly it told us one thing: we’re in for a lot of drama.

Amid old and new cheating scandals, it looks like Katie Maloney and Tom Schwartz are learning how to share.

So who is the pink-haired girl they both kissed in the trailer?

We only see the tiniest bit of Katie Maloney and Tom Schwartz‘s new shared gal pal in the Vanderpump Rules season 11 trailer.

She spends most of her scant few seconds locking lips with both of the divorcees.

In her first appearance, their new girl confesses, “I have a crush on a divorced couple,” to Katie.

We then see a side-by-side shot of her kissing Schwartz and Katie on two different nights.

Of course, Schwartz and Katie have two very different reactions to this.

“I’ve never been in a love triangle before,” Schwartz says.

“May the best man win,” says Katie.

Then, cut to Ms. LVP Lisa Vanderpump herself saying, “Now I see a sh*t storm coming.”

So who exactly is the lucky lady?

Her identity has been revealed as singer-songwriter Tori Keeth, who may be new to the silver screen, but isn’t new to the Vanderpump family.
