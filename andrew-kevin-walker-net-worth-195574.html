Andrew Kevin Walker Net Worth

Andrew Kevin Walker was born on August 14, 1964 in Altoona, PA. He is a renowned screenwriter, best known for writing the screenplay for the 1995 film Seven starring Brad Pitt. His work was so well-received that he was nominated for the BAFTA Award for Best Original Screenplay.
