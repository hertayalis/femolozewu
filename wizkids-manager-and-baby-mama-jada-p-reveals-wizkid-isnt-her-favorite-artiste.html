Wizkids Manager And Baby Mama, Jada P, Reveals Wizkid Isnt Her Favorite Artiste

Jada Pollock, the 3rd baby mama and manager of Nigerian singer, Ayodeji Balogun Ibrahim aka Wizkid has revealed that Wizkid is not her favorite artiste.

During a question and answer session with her fans on Instagram, Jada Pollock was asked about who her favorite artist is and in her response she posted a photo of Jamaican Reggae Legend, Bob Marley.

Her revelation threw fans into a state of shock as Jada P is not only Wizkid ’s baby mama but the one who manages him, however, it is not surprising to us since Bob Marley is loved by almost ever music lover around the world and he is still a hero to many.
