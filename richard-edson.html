Biography

Richard Edson was born in New York on January 1, 1954. Famous as both a musician and an actor, he played drums or horn for the bands Konk and The Offs and appeared in the films Ferris Bueller’s Day Off (as a parking attendant) and Joey Breaker (as the title character). He co-starred with John Lurie in the 1984 comedy movie Stranger Than Paradise. In the early 1980s, he served as Sonic Youth’s first drummer.
