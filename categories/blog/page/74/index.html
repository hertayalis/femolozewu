Are Queen Elizabeth and Her Husband Prince Philip Related?

published on August 13, 2024
How did we not know this?! British monarch Queen Elizabeth and her husband of 70 years, Prince Philip, are actually related. Yes, you read that right! Elizabeth and Philip are second cousins once removed through King Christian IX of Denmark and his wife, Louise of Hesse-Kassel. They’re also third cousins through Queen Victoria and Prince Albert. Interesting… The future Queen first met Prince Philip of Greece and Denmark in 1934 at the royal wedding of Philip’s cousin Princess Marina of Greece and Denmark to Elizabeth’s paternal uncle Prince George, Duke of Kent.
