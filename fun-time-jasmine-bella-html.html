The Mir Fam - Members, Ages, Trivia

Channel featuring primarily family vlogs and challenges. The channel has earned more than 1.4 million subscribers and 659 million total video views. The channel was formerly called Fun-Time with Jasmine and Bella and was previously focused mostly on slime videos and toy reviews. 

Trivia

The channel's first two videos to reach 1 million views were "MYSTERY WHEEL OF SLIME CHALLENGE!" and "Mystery Wheel of Slime Challenge with Switch Up and with 3 Colors of Glue Slime Challenge." The sister's could be found on musical.ly, later TikTok, under the username Funtimewithjb.
