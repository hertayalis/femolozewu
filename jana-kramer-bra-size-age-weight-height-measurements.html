Biography:

Jana Kramer was born on December 2, 1983 in Detroit, Michigan, U.S. She made her acting debut with the low-budget independent horror movie Dead/Undead in 2002. The following year, she made her first television appearance in the soap All My Children.

She has since appeared in other shows including Grey’s Anatomy, Private Practice and CSI: NY. She then became known as Alex Dupre in the teen drama One Tree Hill during its seventh to ninth season.

In film, she has starred in a number of features including Click, Prom Night, The Poker Club, and Spring Breakdown. In 2012, she began her musical career with the single “Why Ya Wanna” from her self-titled debut album.
