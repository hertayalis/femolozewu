Kim Kardashian Shares Picture of Saint Kissing Chicago

Kim Kardashian is a reality TV star, a successful entrepreneur, and a doting mother of three. Her Instagram features some truly aww-inspiring pictures of her adorable kids, North, Saint, and Chicago.

In her latest Instagram, she shows off the love between her son, Saint, and her newborn baby, Chicago.

"He loves her so much," Kim captioned the sweet picture of Saint leaning in to kiss his baby sister lightly on the nose.

Kim loves to share pictures of her kids on Instagram. Like this one of North that Kim captioned mood (we've all been there, too):

And this gorgeous shot that showed off Chicago's adorable face to the world:

Kim and her husband, rapper and designer Kanye West, welcomed Chicago, their third child, via surrogate in January. In a post to her blog, Kim announced Chicago's arrival, writing:

January 15, 201812:47am7lbs 6ozKanye and I are happy to announce the arrival of our healthy, beautiful baby girl. We are incredibly grateful to our surrogate who made our dreams come true with the greatest gift one could give and to our wonderful doctors and nurses for their special care. North and Saint are especially thrilled to welcome their baby sister.Love,Kim Kardashian West

Although Chicago was born via surrogate, Kim and Kanye were in the delivery room to welcome her and Kim was reportedly the the first person to have skin-to-skin contact with the baby.

In early February, as part of Kylie Jenner's tribute video celebrating the birth of her own daughter, Stormi, Kim shared the first glimpse of Chicago with the world.
